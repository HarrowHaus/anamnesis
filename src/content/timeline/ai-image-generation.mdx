DALL·E 2 opened to full public access in September 2022; Midjourney launched open beta in July 2022; Stable Diffusion was released publicly in August 2022. OpenAI's GPT-4o native image generation launched 25 March 2025, and within one week 130 million users had generated over 700 million images. Frontier image generation is now concentrated in a handful of firms — OpenAI (with Microsoft holding approximately 27%), Google DeepMind, Adobe, Midjourney, Black Forest Labs, and xAI — whose training-data biases shape every generated image. Control responses — Google's SynthID watermarking (2023), the C2PA Content Credentials standard, and the EU AI Act's Article 50 (obligations from August 2026) — are the 2020s analogue of the councils, injunctions, and decrees of earlier eras.
