In late 2017, a Reddit user with the screen name "deepfakes" used machine learning to swap celebrity faces into video, building on Ian Goodfellow's 2014 Generative Adversarial Networks. Journalist Samantha Cole reported the phenomenon in Vice/Motherboard on 11 December 2017, giving the technique its name. The capability rapidly moved into fraud, non-consensual imagery, and political disinformation — including an AI-cloned robocall of Joe Biden's voice in the January 2024 New Hampshire primary — and directly challenged the credibility of photographic and video evidence that had served as the foundation of documentary truth since 1839.
