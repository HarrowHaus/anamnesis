French theorist and Situationist Guy Debord published *La Société du spectacle* in 1967, comprising 221 numbered theses arguing that in advanced capitalism, authentic lived life has been replaced by its representation in images. Thesis 4 states: "The spectacle is not a collection of images, but a social relation among people, mediated by images." Debord traces a movement from *being* to *having* to *appearing* — a trajectory that the algorithmic feed has since completed on a planetary scale.
