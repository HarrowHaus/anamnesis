definition: "The slow cyclical wobble of Earth's axis of rotation — completing one full circuit in approximately 25,772 years — first observed by the Greek astronomer Hipparchus around 129 BCE. It causes the zodiac signs to drift from their original constellations and features prominently in astrotheological and astrological systems of cyclical time."
