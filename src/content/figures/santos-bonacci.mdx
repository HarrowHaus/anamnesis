Santos Bonacci built a system out of one claim: that every religion, myth, and fairy tale is the same sky-story told again. His "syncretism" reads scripture as encoded astronomy — the twelve tribes and twelve disciples as the twelve zodiac signs, the cycles of sun, moon, and planets standing beneath the names. Bonacci teaches the Hermetic axiom *as above, so below* as a literal correspondence between the zodiac and the human body, the inner "Christos" regenerated along the spine. Astrology, in his telling, is the hidden mother of all the religions that followed.
