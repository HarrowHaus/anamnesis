Peter Joseph made a film in 2007 that no one in the tradition had managed before: he took the astrotheology thesis — the dense, citation-heavy argument running from Dupuis and Graves through Massey and Maxwell — and cut it into ninety minutes of free online video. Zeitgeist: The Movie's opening section, "The Greatest Story Ever Told," maps the Gospel narrative onto the zodiac and the solar cycle, frame by frame. Joseph stated directly in a recorded interview that Jordan Maxwell was the lifeblood of that section; D.M. Murdock served as consultant and co-produced The Zeitgeist Sourcebook. The result was a transmission event: a lineage that had moved through books, lectures, and shortwave radio suddenly had a billion-view internet document. Nearly every conversation about astrotheology after 2007 passes through this film.
