Mark Passio takes the word *occult* at its root — "hidden" — and builds his work around what has been hidden: Natural Law. He teaches that knowledge is structured like a pyramid, deep understanding concentrated at the top while the base is kept in the dark, and that the mystery traditions first used symbols to teach morality before that same grammar was turned toward control. In Passio's reading the Tarot and the Masonic tracing-boards are moral instruments, not fortune-telling — diagrams of conscience. His standing claim is that government runs on coercion, and on the "order-followers" willing to carry it out.
