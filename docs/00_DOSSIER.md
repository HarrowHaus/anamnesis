## 1. THESIS & POSITIONING

**Core argument.** Whoever controls representation — image, symbol, story, sound — controls the population's interior, because symbols move us *beneath* reason. This is not new: Plato legislated it, every empire fought over it, and modern power has industrialized it. Most people today are saturated with engineered symbols yet cannot read a single one. To wake up is to become literate again.

**Stance.** Advocacy. We present the tradition's case at full strength and take its claims seriously. We do *not* present it as flimsy — every entry is sourced (Section 11), because the strongest version of this case is the well-documented one.

**What we are NOT.** Not a debunk site. Not a "secret cabal of [ethnic group]" site — the antagonist here is *power structures*: state, church, advertising, media, corporate, and now the platforms that own the image-machines. Keep the frame on institutions and methods, never ethnicity. This keeps the site credible, shareable, and un-cancellable.

## 5. THE PILLARS (defined — these are the spine essays)

**P1 — WHO CONTROLS THE IMAGE.** *(The keystone.)*
Plato, *Republic* II–III: the state must censor poets and control which musical modes are permitted, because rhythm/story/image sink beneath reason into the soul. Book X: banish the imitative artists entirely. Thesis: representation is power; whoever owns it owns the interior life of a people.
*Anchors:* Plato (real, citable). *Proponents:* Tsarion. *Examples:* state propaganda, national branding.

**P2 — THE WAR ON IMAGES.** *(The historical proof.)*
The invariant pattern: every power that wants the soul fights over images first. Second Commandment (graven images) → Byzantine Iconoclasm (8th–9th c.) → Islamic aniconism → Protestant whitewashing. Image-control = soul-control, documented across four millennia.
*Anchors:* real religious/art history (highly citable — this is the credibility ballast).

**P3 — ASTROTHEOLOGY & THE SKY-CODE.**
The tradition's central claim: religious figures, holidays, and iconography encode the sun, zodiac, and precession. The "decode scripture as cosmology" thesis.
*Proponents:* Maxwell, Bonacci, Massey, Kuhn, Dupuis/Volney (the historical headwaters). *Frame:* present as the tradition's interpretive system, sourced to proponents; anchor the *history of the idea* (Dupuis 1795) as fact.

**P4 — THE ENGINEERING OF CONSENT.** *(The modern, undeniable layer.)*
Bernays and applied propaganda; advertising as manufactured desire; semiotics of the everyday (Barthes); sensory engineering. This is the documented, present-day face of the same ancient art — and the bridge that makes skeptics nod.
*Anchors:* Bernays *Propaganda*, Barthes *Mythologies*, Vicary subliminal episode. All citable.

**P5 — SYMBOLIC ILLITERACY & THE AI IMAGE AGE.** *(The 2026 capstone — the wedge.)*
We are the most image-saturated, symbol-illiterate population in history. Emoji and brand-glyphs are a hieroglyphics nobody's taught to read. Now AI image-generators put the entire visual unconscious under the control of whoever owns the models — Plato's nightmare, fully industrialized. *No other site frames it this way yet.*
*Anchors:* current — to be researched (Mission 5).

## 11. SOURCING & CREDIBILITY STANDARD (non-negotiable)

Every claim carries a source, tagged by tier:
- **Tier A — Hard history / scholarship.** Plato, art history, Bernays, Jung, Barthes, Yates, academic works. *Stated as fact.*
- **Tier B — The figures' own works.** Maxwell/Tsarion/Bonacci books & lectures. *Stated as "X argues…" — attributed, not asserted as settled fact.*
- **Tier C — Secondary / community sources.** Use sparingly, only to point to A or B.

Rule: a believer's site is only powerful if it's *credible*. Attribute interpretive claims; assert only what's documentable. This is what separates ANAMNESIS from the 10,000 forgettable "wake up" sites.

- **Reading level:** accessible to a curious newcomer; depth available on click-through.
