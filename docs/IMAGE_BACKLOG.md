# Image backlog — entries without a verified plate

Entries that could not be given a real, license-verified public-domain / CC0
(or CC-BY-with-credit) plate during Phase E2. Each falls back to the
transitional seed plate and is listed here for follow-up. **Nothing ships with
an unverified licence** (E2/E3 legal gate).

## Reasons (shorthand)
- **TM** — modern trademark/logo; not reproducible as a free image (recreate or
  describe per `docs/05` §65). Most `corporate` symbols + modern-logo casebook
  artifacts.
- **LIVING** — living figure; no freely-licensed portrait found.
- **NOFREE** — no public-domain/CC0 image of the actual subject located yet.
- **UNCLEAR** — a candidate exists but its licence could not be confirmed; do
  not ship until verified.
